Why Support Us?

COVID-19 has caused a huge increase in the amount of people suffering from mental health issues.

11% of funding is dedicated to mental health trusts, despite mental health illnesses taking up 23% of all NHS activity.

75% of people who are suffering with mental health difficulties do not receive any support.

Stigma is still associated with people who are suffering from mental health difficulties.

Anti-depressant prescriptions have doubled over the last 10 years, and continues to rise.

1/4 people will be affected by some kind of mental health disorder at some point in their life.

In Leicester alone;

25% of children say they have experienced bullying within the last 12 months.

30% of children and younger people find it hard to trust other indivuals.

20% of younger people
regularly feel sad, down and disheartened.
